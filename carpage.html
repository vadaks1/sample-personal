Car Maintenance

Maintaining your car is important to keep it running smoothly. Here are some basic tips:

  • Check your tire pressure regularly
  • Change your oil every 5,000 miles
  • Replace your air filter every 12,000 miles
  • Rotate your tires every 6,000 to 8,000 miles
  • Check your brake pads and replace them when necessary
